Privacy Policy for Everpulse

Effective Date: September 2, 2025

Last Updated: September 2, 2025

Everbolt Engineering (Pvt) Ltd (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Everpulse (“App”) collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number (if provided).
  • Device Information: Device model, OS, unique identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location if granted.
  • Usage Data: Screens accessed, features used, interaction logs.
  • Files & Media: Uploaded invoices, reports, or images (if chosen).
  • Firebase Data: Firebase Authentication & FCM for login & push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve app functionality.
  • Personalize user experience.
  • Enable login, authentication, and account management.
  • Send important updates & notifications.
  • Process uploaded files (e.g., invoices, reports).
  • Monitor and enhance security and reliability.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data only with trusted service providers (e.g., Firebase, Google Maps API), or if required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures such as encryption and secure servers. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

Everpulse uses third-party services like Google Firebase and Google Maps API. These have their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and update your data.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Withdraw location/notification permissions.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. If we become aware of such data collection, we delete it.

9. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will appear here with a new “Last Updated” date.
